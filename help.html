You can run the calculator offline by unzipping the following archive and opening index.html in your browser:

heliocalc.zip

To use the 'Open', 'Save', 'Import' and 'Export' functions offline, you need to simulate a webserver on your local system. The easiest way to do this is by installing python on your system, opening a text terminal, navigating to the heliocalc directory, and typing python -m SimpleHTTPServer'. The calculator can then be accessed through your browser at http://localhost:8000
